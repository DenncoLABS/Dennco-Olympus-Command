# Olympus Infrastructure

Olympus Infrastructure is a draft app pack built from the Dennco Olympus Command master platform.

## Purpose

Roads, bridges, water systems, power assets, telecom routes, public facilities, maintenance, inspection records, project status, and infrastructure risk awareness.

## Likely users

- Infrastructure planners
- Public works departments
- Facilities operators
- Utility or telecom operations teams
- Executive viewers

## Starting modules

- Earth/map asset view
- Infrastructure asset registry
- Inspection and maintenance records
- DOT and road-condition layers
- Weather and hazard overlays
- Risk scoring
- Infrastructure status reports

## App boundary

This app should organize authorized infrastructure information and public-source context. Sensitive facility or critical infrastructure details should be access-controlled and deployment-specific.

## Status

Draft planning scaffold. Not production-ready.
