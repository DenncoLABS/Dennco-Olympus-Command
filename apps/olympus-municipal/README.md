# Olympus Municipal

Olympus Municipal is a draft app pack built from the Dennco Olympus Command master platform.

## Purpose

Municipal operations, public works, civic infrastructure, road conditions, local alerts, public meeting awareness, grant tracking, and city/county operational intelligence.

## Likely users

- City administrators
- Public works directors
- Emergency managers
- Infrastructure planners
- Council or board staff
- Executive viewers

## Starting modules

- Earth/map screen
- DOT traffic and road conditions
- Infrastructure overlays
- Public notices and meeting feeds
- Weather and hazard awareness
- Reports and executive briefs
- Admin-controlled source registry

## App boundary

This app should use public, authorized, and organization-approved municipal information. It should not become a private-person surveillance product.

## Status

Draft planning scaffold. Not production-ready.
