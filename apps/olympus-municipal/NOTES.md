# Olympus Municipal Notes

## Planning status

Draft. This app pack exists to collect municipal-specific direction without pushing municipal-only assumptions into the Olympus core.

## Research questions

1. Which municipal customer type comes first: city, county, township, public works, or executive office?
2. Which public data sources are most useful first?
3. Should the first MVP focus on roads, budget/public meetings, emergency awareness, or infrastructure projects?
4. Which reports should be generated first?
5. What privacy and public-records boundaries must be documented?

## Candidate source lanes

- Public meeting agendas and minutes
- Road closure and traffic feeds
- Public works project data
- Grant notices
- Weather and hazards
- Public budgets and financial reports
- Infrastructure maps where public/authorized

## Developer reminder

Municipal-specific behavior should start inside this app pack, a mirror, or an extension. Do not hard-code municipal assumptions into core unless the feature benefits multiple app packs.
