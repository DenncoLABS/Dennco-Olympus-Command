# Olympus Emergency Management

Olympus Emergency Management is a draft app pack built from the Dennco Olympus Command master platform.

## Purpose

Hazard awareness, weather alerts, incident coordination, shelter/resource visibility, road closures, utility impacts, public alerts, and recovery status.

## Likely users

- Emergency managers
- Municipal or county operations staff
- Public safety coordinators
- Public works leaders
- Executive viewers

## Starting modules

- Weather and hazard layers
- Incident feed
- CAD-linked operational notes
- Road closure and utility impact views
- Shelter/resource registry
- Situation reports
- Recovery status dashboard

## App boundary

This app should support lawful emergency coordination and situational awareness. It should not create autonomous response actions or replace official emergency command authority.

## Status

Draft planning scaffold. Not production-ready.
