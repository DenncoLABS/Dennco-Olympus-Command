Powered Desk hatch trigger.

Changes:
- Olympus Desk now operates like a powered hatch with latched, opening, open, and closing states.
- Clicking the hatch latch manually operates the Desk.
- Dock buttons automatically unlatch and raise the Desk.
- Desk slowly rises and slowly closes/latches.
- Idle Desk closes and latches after a timeout.
- Obsolete fixed Dock override was neutralized so dragging/latching remains functional.
